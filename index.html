We've got what you need!

Our company is a well-established business that specializes in providing high-quality paint, pipe fittings, waterproof chemicals, tile adhesive, and door security products. We have been in the industry for many years and have built a reputation for offering a wide range of products at competitive prices, as well as providing expert advice and excellent customer service.

We originally started as a B2B (business-to-business) company, supplying products to other businesses and contractors. However, over the years, we have expanded our offerings and have become a B2C (business-to-consumer) company, catering to individual home-owners and DIY enthusiasts.

We currently employ a total of 9 dedicated and knowledgeable employees, who are committed to providing our customers with the best possible shopping experience. They are always available to assist with any questions or concerns you may have and provide expert recommendations.
